4.2 Algorithms

Release 0: Equip your new robot

- When the elevator door closes
	- Ask "What floor?"
		- If a response is not heard
			- Ask "What floor?" 
		- Otherwise:
			- Press the floor button corresponding response


Release 1: Find a Real-Life Algorithm

- For each kid listed in the household
	- When time reaches 7:30am from Monday to Friday
		- Until you hear a response, or until you have attempted three times:
			- Call out the kid's name followed by "wake up"
			- Wait three seconds for a response.
		- If no response was heard:
			- Mark the kid sick.
